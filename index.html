Principles · 我们的原则

Three things we won't do.

  1. 01

    We won't think for you.

    The apps ask; you answer. No AI verdicts, no “you should,” no conclusions handed to you. 不替你思考。

  2. 02

    We won't manufacture anxiety.

    No outrage feed. No streak guilt. No red badges screaming for your attention. 不制造焦虑。

  3. 03

    We won't sell mystical growth.

    No horoscopes, no “unlock your true potential.” Just two honest tools that respect your time. 不卖玄学成长。

Plans & privacy
