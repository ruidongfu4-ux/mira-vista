1. What Vista is

Vista is a news comprehension and reflection tool that uses AI to help you understand what's happening in the world and train your read on events. It is:

  • A daily reader — curated news cards from public RSS sources, with short summaries and AI analysis
  • A questioner — Today's Five Questions, Daily Question, and per-article Q&A help you think, not scroll

Vista is not:
